Tendril (48hr GGJ23)

January 29th, 2023


Links: Itch.io

Description

2D Metroidvania-esque game within the Global Game Jam of 2023. This follows the prompt of 'Roots', and we interpreted this as physically being rooted to the inside of a giant monster. We also used 'going back to your roots', by dragging the player back to the start if they did not finish the game in time.

We were too ambitious with this project, as we did not get to add our main loop idea of saving positions, and as such the game must be completed fully in one run. This also lead to gameplay and map design being created, without being put into the game.

My Contributions

  • Dynamically Attached Tendril which extends by force
  • Health Points Management
  • Win Condition

The King Returns (48hr GMTK23)

July 10th, 2023


Links: Itch.io

Description

Anti Hack-n-Slash within the GMTK game jam of 2023. The theme was roles reversed, and we interpreted this by inverting the role of the player in a traditional Hack-n-Slash. Instead of playing as a weak character trying to kill NPCs, you are an incredibly strong character who is trying to stop the NPC from killing themselves by fighting you. You play as the King of a dedicated town of people who are willing to die to protect their kingdom. The people do not recognise their king anymore, and will ram the player trying to stop them from getting into the kingdom. The player is extremely overpowered, and just by simply walking into these NPCs, will they die.

The player is given abilities which allow him to stun his people to prevent them from dying to him, the players health bar is actually the healthbar of all people in his kingdom, as when the last townsperson dies, the king loses his kingdom. The player must progress by collecting downgrades, to make himself as weak as possible. However, as he gets closer to the center of his kingdom, he will encounter people who have never seen battle before, and as such are even weaker than before.
